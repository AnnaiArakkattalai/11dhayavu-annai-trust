Our Ecological Philosophy

Reversing Global Warming by Restoring Native Canopies.

At Dhayavu Annai Arakkattalai Trust, we reject short-term cosmetic greening. We believe that saving nature requires strategic, scientific, and community-owned forestry frameworks. We carefully study local topsoil degradation profiles and introduce diverse native species to foster stable plant guilds that anchor real resilience.

By integrating nearby agrarian families into our logistics pipelines, we supply sustained employment opportunities while ensuring that every forest belt remains strictly protected from early stage threats.

Endemic Species Safeguard

We harvest, germinate, and plant strict native species like Neem, Pungan, and Peepal to promote insect and avian lifecycle protection.

Water Basin Recharging

Targeted dense planting along drying river networks dramatically enhances subterranean soil water holding capacities.
